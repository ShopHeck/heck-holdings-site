How it works

Not a Zapier template. Actual engineering.

We don't hand you a list of Zaps and wish you luck. We architect proper integrations using APIs, webhooks, and middleware — with error handling, retry logic, and monitoring so things don't silently break at 2 AM.

Week 1

Workflow mapping & tool audit

We map every tool in your stack, identify which workflows eat the most time, and design the integration architecture. You get a visual diagram showing exactly how AI will flow through your existing tools — which data goes where, what triggers what, and where humans stay in the loop. Fixed scope, fixed price before we write a line of code.

Week 2

Build & connect

We wire up the integrations using official APIs (not screen scraping), configure the AI models with your specific context and writing style, build the automation triggers, and set up error handling and logging. Every integration is tested against your real data — not demo accounts.

Week 3

Test, train & go live

We run the integrations in shadow mode — AI processes real work but you review everything before it sends. Once you're confident, we flip to live. Your team gets a training walkthrough and a runbook. We stick around for 14 days to tune prompts and catch edge cases.
