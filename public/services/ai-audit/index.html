Your week

Five days, start to finish.

No meetings-about-meetings. Every day has a clear purpose and a visible output. You'll never wonder what we're doing.

Day 01–02

Discovery & observation

We embed with your team. Watch the real workflows. Interview the people doing the work — not just the managers who think they know. We map every tool, handoff, and bottleneck. By end of day two, we know your operations better than most employees.

Day 03

Analysis & scoring

We score every workflow against our automation framework — effort, impact, risk, and dependencies. The opportunity scorecard takes shape. We identify the quick wins, the high-impact projects, and the things that aren't worth automating (there are always a few).

Day 04

Architecture & planning

We design the agent architecture, map integrations to your existing stack, build the ROI model with real numbers, and draft your 90-day implementation roadmap. Every recommendation is specific to your business — not generic best practices.

Day 05

Deliverable & walkthrough

You get the full audit package — all six deliverables — and a live walkthrough where we explain every recommendation. Every question answered. You leave knowing exactly what to build first and what it's worth. If nothing's worth building, we'll tell you that too.
