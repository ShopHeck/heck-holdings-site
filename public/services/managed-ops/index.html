Why managed ops

The deploy is not the finish line.

Most AI projects die after launch. The agent ships, everyone celebrates, and then — slowly — things drift. The LLM provider updates their model. Your CRM changes an API field. A new edge case appears that nobody anticipated. The agent starts making mistakes nobody notices for weeks.

This isn't a failure of engineering. It's a failure of operations. AI agents aren't "set it and forget it" — they're living systems that need ongoing attention, just like the people they replaced.

Managed Operations means you don't have to hire an ML engineer or build an internal ops team to keep your agents healthy. We've already built the tooling, the processes, and the muscle memory. You get all of it for a flat monthly fee.

How it works

A monthly rhythm
your team can count on.

Daily

Continuous monitoring

Every agent action is logged and monitored. Automated alerts trigger on anomalies — unusual error rates, latency spikes, accuracy drops. We investigate and resolve before your team ever notices. You see a clean dashboard; we see the sausage factory.

Weekly

Performance brief

A short, written brief lands in your inbox (or Slack) every week. What your agents processed, what they escalated, what improved, what needs attention. Think of it as a one-page employee performance review — for your agents.

Monthly

Improvement cycle

Each month, we ship improvements based on the data. Prompt refinements, new edge case handling, eval updates, integration adjustments. We present what we changed, why, and what impact it had. Your agents aren't static — they compound.

Quarterly

Strategic review

Every quarter, we step back and look at the big picture. What's working well. What new automations are worth building. How the agent landscape has changed and whether there are new opportunities. This is where the next audit recommendations often emerge.
