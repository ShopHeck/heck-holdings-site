How it works

A clear path from
"we should look into AI" to production.

Most engagements follow the same arc. You don't have to commit to all three — the audit stands on its own. But here's how the pieces fit together.

Week 1

Audit your operations

We shadow your team, map every workflow, interview the people doing the work, and score every process against our automation framework. You get a full audit package with ROI math and a prioritized build plan. If nothing's worth automating, we'll tell you — and you don't owe us a follow-up engagement.

Weeks 2–8

Build your first agent

Starting with the highest-ROI opportunity from your audit, we design and build a production agent on your existing stack. Real integrations, real data, real evals. You watch progress in a shared Linear board and review every milestone. The agent ships with an eval harness, observability dashboard, and a runbook so your team isn't dependent on us.

Ongoing

We run it — you focus on growth

Agents in production need monitoring, tuning, and continuous improvement. We handle all of it on a flat monthly retainer. You get weekly briefs showing what your agents did, what improved, and what's coming next. When something breaks at 2 AM, we're the ones who wake up.

Who this is for

Small businesses with big operations.

We work best with teams of 5–200 who are already productive but losing hours to repetitive work. If your team is great at what they do but drowning in process, we should talk.

Law firms, agencies, consultancies — anyone whose team spends more time on intake, scheduling, and admin than on billable work.

E-commerce operators

Brands processing hundreds of orders, refunds, and vendor invoices per week. If your bookkeeper is your bottleneck, we can fix that.

Trades & field service

Contractors and service companies who need estimates out the door faster, scheduling locked down, and after-hours calls answered.

Growing teams

Any business that's about to hire for a role that's mostly process. Before you post the job listing, ask if an agent could do 80% of it.
