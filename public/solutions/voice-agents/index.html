How it works

Pick up. Qualify. Book. Confirm.

Ring

Answer in seconds

FrontDesk picks up within 2 rings. The voice is warm, natural, and trained on your business — it greets callers with your company name, follows your brand tone, and immediately sounds like part of your team. Callers can't tell it's AI.

Qualify

Understand what they need

The agent asks clarifying questions naturally — service type, location, urgency, budget range. It handles FAQs (hours, pricing ballparks, service areas) without needing to transfer. Multi-turn conversation, not a phone tree.

Book

Schedule on the spot

With real-time access to your calendar (Cal.com, Google Calendar, Acuity), FrontDesk offers available slots and books the appointment while the caller is still on the phone. No "someone will call you back." The appointment is confirmed before they hang up.

Follow up

Confirm via SMS

Immediately after the call, the caller gets an SMS confirmation with appointment details, address, and any prep instructions. Your team gets a Slack notification with the full call summary, classification, and recording link.
