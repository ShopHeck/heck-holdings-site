The problem

Proposals are the bottleneck.

Field-service businesses live and die by their proposals. But creating one means driving back to the office, pulling up a template, manually entering measurements and photos, looking up material prices, running the math, formatting the PDF, and sending it. That's 1–3 hours per proposal — and the customer who gets the first proposal usually wins the job.

ProposalForge compresses that to 90 seconds. Your field tech snaps photos, dictates a quick brief, and a branded proposal lands in the customer's inbox before they've left the site. First mover advantage, every time.

How it works

Snap. Speak. Send.

Capture

Photos + voice brief

Your field tech photographs the job site and records a 30-second voice note: "Three-bedroom house, needs full interior repaint, walls are in fair condition, two accent walls, customer wants Benjamin Moore." That's all ProposalForge needs.

Analyze

Vision + pricing engine

Vision AI analyzes the photos — room dimensions, surface conditions, obstacles. The pricing engine pulls current material costs, applies your labor rates, calculates totals, and adds your margin. Everything cross-referenced against your historical pricing data.

Generate

Branded PDF proposal

A professionally formatted, branded PDF proposal is generated with line items, photos, scope of work, timeline, terms, and e-signature fields. It looks like your best proposal — because it was trained on your best proposals.

Deliver

Send & track

The proposal is emailed to the customer and logged in your CRM. A DocuSign link is included for immediate signing. You get a notification when they open it, when they view each page, and when they sign. Follow-up reminders are automated.
