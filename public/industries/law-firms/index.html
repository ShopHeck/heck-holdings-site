The reality

Where law firms leak time.

A typical mid-size firm has 3–5 attorneys, a paralegal or two, and an office manager. Everyone is doing administrative work that doesn't generate revenue: fielding intake calls, writing meeting recaps, formatting engagement letters, researching opposing counsel, and chasing down retainer agreements. That's 10–15 hours per attorney per week that could be billable.

The bottleneck isn't talent. It's the operational overhead that scales with every new client. AI agents eliminate the overhead without eliminating the human judgment that makes your firm valuable.
