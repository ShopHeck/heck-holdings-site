The reality

The agency coordination tax.

A 10-person agency runs 25+ client meetings per week. Each meeting generates action items, client commitments, and context that needs to be distributed, tracked, and recalled months later. Meanwhile, the new business pipeline needs proposals for every qualified lead — and each proposal takes 4–8 hours to build from scratch.

The result: your strategists spend 30% of their time on admin. Your account managers spend half their day on email. Your founders spend evenings writing proposals. And nobody has time to monitor what competitors are doing — until a client asks "why is [competitor] offering this?"

AI agents break this pattern. Not by replacing your team — by giving them back the hours that should be spent on client work, strategy, and creative.
